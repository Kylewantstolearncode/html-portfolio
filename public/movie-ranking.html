The Best Movies According to Kyle

My Top Movies of All Time

La La Land

A deep dive into the cost of ambition and the hardships of chasing your dreams diguised as a simple romance. Lead by relatable underdog protagonists and takes some daring risks with its narrative. Of course, the visuals and music are incredible as well

Bladerunner 2049

A slowburn sci-fi film that focuses less on flashy action sequences and more on discussing the concept of humanity. The question: "What makes someone human?" is asked through the view point of several fascinating inhuman characters. This is all wrapped around a movie with gorgeous cinematography

Whisper of the Heart

A gorgeously animated slice of life film that follows a young girl trying to find her place in life. Most might find this film to be mundane, but I think the slow pacing and simple narrative enhances the exploration of its themes.
